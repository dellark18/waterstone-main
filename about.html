About Us

Waterstone Geo-services Ltd. is a leading geoscience and borehole drilling consultant based in Nigeria established to address the need of water, collapse buildings and environmental problem such as contaminated areas in order to increase the awareness of the subsurface investigation and reduce water challenges in the most needed part of Nigeria and Africa at large.

Waterstone has an experience and talented team on hydrogeological surveys and borehole drilling. We were incorporated and registered in Nigeria by the Corporate Affairs Commission. Our solutions as an organization aimed at four key business areas: Water, infrastructure, Environmental and Natural resources with applications as varied as groundwater prospecting, civil engineering and archaeology.

We offer a full turnkey solution to customers from project planning all the way to final completion of a project. Our geophysical surveys are always carried out by highly qualified geologists and geophysicists. This allows problem to be solved on- site by customizing data acquisition to the highest level of the technical excellence.

Our expertise in Geophysical surveys and borehole drilling places us at the forefront of the industry in Nigeria. We are readily available to offer our professional services to all Africa region in diaspora.
